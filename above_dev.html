Take to the service of Lord Rama


and Volunteer for Sri Rama Navami

Let Sri Rama be your ideal. Ideals are remembered and adored for the purpose of adopting them in your own life. Lord Rama was the prince of the Ikshvaku race. He was virtuous Lord of the mind and the senses. Brave and valiant, and yet He was gentle and modest. He was a sage in counsel, kind and sweet in speech, and most courteous and handsome in appearance. He was the master of the weapons, and a great warrior. Ever devoted to the good and prosperity of His kingdom and His subjects, He was a defender of the weak and the protector of the righteous. He was well versed in the science of the Self.

One cannot be a real adorer of Lord Rama unless one makes an honest attempt to grow in the virtues that the Lord represents. The Ramnavmi celebration or the Vasanta Navaratri every year is an opportune period for us to saturate ourselves with the spirit of Lord Rama.
