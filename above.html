Srivari Maha Samprokshana Mahotsavam

    Sri Venkateswara Temple of North Carolina is entering twelfth year since the pratishta by Sri Tridandi Srimannarayana Ramanuja Chinna Jeeyar Swamy. On this auspicious occasion Maha Samprokshana Pushkarokshavam will be conducted as defined by the agama shastras. Sri Swami varu was in ‘Vaamana’ form in the baala aalayam till 2009, upon prana pratishta he has been blessing us in the form of ‘Trivikrama’. He again wishes to transform into ‘Vaamana’ form to allow us to conduct necessary repairs, homas to purify and maintain the sanctity of the Temple.
