Historic Nancy Jones House moving

Dear Devotees,

The historic Nancy Jones House currently located on the south-east side of Temple property is scheduled to be moved to another nearby location on Saturday, March 20, 2021.  Please note all preparations for the move will begin starting March 5th.  The details of the move will be shared once it is finalized.

Kindly pay attention to all signs and  directions for visiting the temple on the said day.  Please expect road closures and alternate routes for entering and exiting the Temple premises on that day.

It is crucial we adhere to all necessary rules to accommodate for a successful move on this date.  Many parties are involved including Duke Energy, AT&T, Contractor Ben Hale, Wolfe House & Building Movers, the NC Department of Transportation, Cary Police Department, and the Cary Fire Department, and it is imperative we follow directions for everyone's safety. Appreciate your cooperation and patience in this matter.

Thanking you in advance for your co-operation


Timing Changes

Dear Devotees, With the night-curfew cleared by NC Governor Roy Cooper, beginning April 2021 the temple closing timings will fall-back to the regular closing timings of 9 pm Eastern.  Kindly check the timing changes announcements before you visit the temple.
